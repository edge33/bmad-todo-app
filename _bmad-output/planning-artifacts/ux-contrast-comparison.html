Contrast Comparison

Original Issue: Completed tasks blend too much with the gradient background and reduced opacity makes them feel secondary.

What We're Testing: Three approaches to increase contrast and make completed tasks feel distinct and satisfying.

Option 1: Rich Green

Vibrant, high-contrast green. Makes completed tasks pop. Feels celebratory and accomplished.

#10b981

Option 2: Medium Green

Softer green with shadow depth. Still feels happy but more refined. Easy on the eyes.

#34d399

Option 3: Warm Beige

Completely different color. Signals "this is different." Feels warm and settled.

Which Contrast Approach Feels Right?

Each option makes completed tasks more visible and satisfying, but with different emotional tones:

  • Option 1 - Bold and celebratory. "You did it!"
  • Option 2 - Balanced and refined. Clear but not too loud.
  • Option 3 - Warm and settled. "This is complete and safe."
